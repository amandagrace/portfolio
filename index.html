sydney-based ui.ux designer

b.s. computational media, georgia tech

I was that kid who always asked how and why. Some of us grow out of this incessant questioning. The rest of us become designers. As a user experience designer, I ask the right questions to untangle the ideas and messages in my clients' heads and turn them into simple, effective experiences for their users.

Experiences are everywhere, and I love finding them. It's why I love to travel, interact with all sorts of media, and try new things. I learn from the successes and failures of the experiences around me – even the failures of some of my own designs – to improve my work.

And thanks to my interdisciplinary degree from Georgia Tech, I understand the technologies I design for. I have a strong foundation in visual, interactive, and information design theory, as well as extensive programming coursework. I can prototype and develop what I design.

If you'd like to get to know me better, I'd love to chat. Feel free to reach out!
